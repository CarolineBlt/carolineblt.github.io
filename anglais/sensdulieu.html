Film photographs
305 x 405 mm
Plastic expression
April 2016

I took a series of analog photographs of an old building, from which the only remaining thing standing was the door. I then realised a slate engraving, conveying the notions of passage, opening and double point of view. In order to present my work to people, I created an entire set up around spatial problematics (framing, point of view, focused sight, outlying sight). To finish, I created three books, echoing my project by their format, colours and content so as to make a report of my project.

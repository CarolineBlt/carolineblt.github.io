Textile
435 x 1450 mm
Plastic expression
November 2016

I took my inspiration in the traditional bestiary from the Middle Age. I created a piece of inprinted fabric that could be worn as a ornamental cape. Three different patterns can be seen, interweaving on the material. The two first are a pattern on the entire cape. A paving a bird silhouettes transferred thanks to acetone is the first one. The second is a linocut blue eagle, realised according to medieval drawing. The third patter is an orange peacock, directly painted onto the fabric.

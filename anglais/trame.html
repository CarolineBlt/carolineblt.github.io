Edition
185 x 245 mm
Plastic expression
December 2015

My starting point was a personal photograph of the attraction « It’s a small world » from the theme park Disneyland Paris. I then created an editorial notebook transcribing the obsessive patterns and weft. The reading of this book becomes playful and full of surprises as we go because I used many and several printing methods (acetone transfer, stencils, lino-cut). This work is about childhood, in a chromatic way.

Edition
107 x 177 mm
Creation studio
March 2017

In the Aubiographie des objects, a book written by François Bon, I chose an extract focusing on the mirror. I used this passage with another book De l’autre côté du miroir from Lewis Caroll. I re-edited the text from Lewis Carroll with the exact same paging as the Gallimard edition. I then justified the text with the second extract from François Bon, but I reversed it according to a vertical axis. This new book can be read in two ways, the normal one or by using a mirror to see the other passage.

Costume, performance and poster
1 scale
Creation studio
February 2016

After I analysed the work of April Greiman, I deduced important principles from it : the elongated format, the presence of a grid, wired elements, the importance of the colour white and of the weft and finally the computer modification of images. I then created a costume using all of the above. I staged it in a poster, resembling a poster that Greiman did for Design Quaterly. A performance also took place in which I wore the costume. During the performance I would spin around on the music « Janitor » from the Suburban Laws band. The wind would blow in my costume and in my hair and I also had a Macintosh from the 80’s with me as well as an image of a Californian beach in the background.

Caroline Bultey

Graphic Designer


Curious, I am always wanting to experiment with new techniques to keep learning new things all the time. Forms are the main thing that lead my experiments. I like to manipulate and work with my hands which is a crucial step in creation according to me. My work is focused on colours and images in order to create polished forms, with attention to details.

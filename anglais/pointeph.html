Poster
400 x 600 mm
Creation studio
November 2016

Wanting to imagine a whole new identity for the centre of Parisian art called « Le Point Ephémère » – which is a centre that organizes concerts and day or night exhibitions – I realised a series of posters, business cards and a calendar. The spots I created manually using an air-brush give the idea of a projector that hides but also highlights the faces of the artists. The round and progressive shape of these spots also reminds us of the centre’s name. They imply an explosion, a diffusion. In the background, there are photographs of the artists, in a negative style in order to get a «rock» vibe, a less institutional sense, just the way Le Point Ephémère is.

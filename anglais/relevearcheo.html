Edition
145 x 205 mm
Plastic expression
October 2016

I dealt with my bathroom as an archaeological site. I drew sketches of various natures, from scientific and archaeological to fast drawings . There are individual sheets about each drawing or they are assembled thanks to a metallic binding. Their classification has been well thought out, from a far away view to locate the bathroom to a closer view of every detail. The room can then be discovered with precision. A sensible and olfactory dimension is brought to the viewer thanks to prints and samples of the room, which can be seen in the annex.

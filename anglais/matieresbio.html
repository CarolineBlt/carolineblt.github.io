Video
30 seconds
Plastic expression
September 2016

I used an empty film that I painted using watercolour and that I re-filmed numerically. With this film, I isolated a particularly interesting segment, interesting because of its plastic and semantics qualities in the matter of biologic material. Then, playing with the endless possibilities of montage, I realised a 30 seconds video called « Collision ». This video is about the confrontation and the encounter of two bodies which when merging give birth to a new element.

Vinyl box and edition
305 x 305 mm
Creation studio
March 2016

After hearing about the Voyager Golden Record, a vinyl that was sent into space in order to let aliens know that we exist (only if they exist as well), I decided to create my own Golden Voyageur. My main theme was « fingerprints ». I made a thermoformed lid using a plaster mould of my hand and also used some laser cut plywood. Thanks to various sockets, a part is embedded into the plywood and allows us to see a black hand at the back of the box. The work is composed of negative facsimiles from the book La ressemblance par contact from George Didi-Huberman. The vinyl macaroons are also negative photographs, but of of my hands. This chromatic inversion is reference to the black and white hands found in prehistoric caves.

Typography
Workshop of 3 days
November 2015

During a workshop with Sarah Kremer and Thomas Bouville, two typographers, my class and I drew a typographic letter, which would be used on the front of the academic inspection of the department Nièvres. The slogan « Liberté, égalité, Fraternité », was written using features from didone typography. However, we modernized them, in order for them to suit their purpose. We scanned them and afterwards we finalized their setting up on the front of the building.
